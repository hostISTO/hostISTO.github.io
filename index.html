About ISTO

Founded in January 2001, ISTO provides independent, worldwide tests of understanding of the ISO 9000:2000 series. To-date, ISTO offers a comprehensive range of qualifications to international standards and other competence areas to address needs of our client's community and professionals from different disciplines.

 Our Mission

To provide independent and impartial, cost-effective, respected and globally recognized qualifications in international standards or other competence areas to individuals around the globe.

 Our Purpose

To enhance the effectiveness of ISO management system standards implementation by offering global benchmark qualifications in the understanding and implementation of the standards.

 What does an ISTO qualification mean?

‣  an opportunity for professionals to demonstrate their knowledge and skills
‣  a platform to acquire qualifications in the understanding and implementation of the standards and other competence areas
‣  satisfies the often-required criteria in the selection of staff members
‣  a basis for the responsible dissemination of knowledge by training organizations
‣  a validating tool on continuing professional development

ISTO Services

Corporates and Companies

‣ The ISTO test provides an impartial and cost-effective way to verify the level of understanding of your technical personnel in the respective ISO management system standards. 

‣ A key driver of a lean, but effective implementation of the ISO management system standards is to understand the distinction between what is required and what is not required. We puts this to the test.

‣ Your organization can use the ISTO Test of Understanding certificate as a criteria in staff selection

‣ With the ISTO Test of Understanding qualification, your staff will contribute better to a tailored system and on-going improvement for your organization.

Individuals

‣ The ISTO Test of Understanding is an opportunity to demonstrate your level of understanding in the respective ISO management system standards.

‣ The ISTO Certificate of Achievement is an unparalleled certification of a strong understanding in management.

‣ The ISO 9000:2000 series is often a selection criteria at corporates and comapnies. ISTO provides the quickiest online method for taking these tests.

‣ You will receive personalized reports that pinpoint your strengths and points to improve after taking the ISTO test.
