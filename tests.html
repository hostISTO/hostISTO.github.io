Test Registration Process

Please have your credit card and your passport photo (in JPG format : max resolution 640 x 480 to 1920 x 1080 file size n.m MB) ready. The process consist of 5 steps:

    1. Personal details

    2. Test type/Test date/Test centre

    3. Agreement to terms and conditions

    4. Payment

    5. Completion (printing of test vouch)

All candidates shall note that they are required to produce a government-issued photo-bearing identification document (passport, identify card, driving license etc) during their check in at the Computer Based Test Centre. They must ensure that the format and spelling of their names is identical to such official document.

Malpractices

ISTO reserves the right to deny or withdraw any registration without giving any reason. In such case, ISTO will fully refund the candidate.

Test Rescheduling

A candidate can reschedule a test to a maximum of two times. The test must be taken within 12 months since the original scheduled date. (that is, taken on or before the same month next year). There will be an admin fee equal to 20% of the nominal test fee.

A candidate can login their own account to reschedule at their convenience.

Missing a Test 

Candidates are required to reschedule the test 48 hours before the test. If a candidate failed to sit for a test, the candidate will be considered as failed.

They can register to re-take the test at their discretion.

Re-taking a Test

Candidates will be given their test results at the center immediately after submitting their test(s). Candidates that are not successful may consider to re-take the test. Candidates will be offered a 50% discount if they re-take the same test, unlimited number of times. Candidates can simply login their account to register.
